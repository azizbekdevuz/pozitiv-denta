Offered Dentistry

At our dental clinic, we offer a full spectrum of affordable
dental, surgical, and orthodontic services. Our offered dentistry includes:
Therapeutic Dentistry: - focuses on treating dental diseases
and conditions and includes procedures such as fillings,
root canals, and gum disease treatment.
Orthopedic Dentistry - concerned with the diagnosis,
prevention, and treatment of dental and facial irregularities.
It includes procedures such as braces and other
orthodontic appliances.
Surgical Dentistry - involves procedures that require
surgery, such as tooth extractions, dental implants, and
jaw surgery.
